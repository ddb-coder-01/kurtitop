Kurti and Top

Comparing a traditional Kurti and a casual Top

Kurti

This kurti is of medium pink coluor. It is a XL sized long kurti. It is round necked kurti. The sleeves are three quarter. The kurti has 2 buttons and some printed texture all over.
It is a very nice traditional wear for girls. It can be wore in some celebrations. It is best suited with palazzo or with leggings also. You can take a dupatta along with it if you want to. Choose some contrastive colors for the bootom and the dupatta.
The price of this beautiful kurti is Rs.250. You may get it at a quite lesser or greater price, as the retailer will be selling.

Top

This top is of bright yellow colour. It is a XL sized top. It is round necked. It has very beautiful bell sleeves. It has a lace tuck in a tie like manner. It has two very beutiful flowers sewed in red and blue colours with some more thread textures on the left shoulder.
It is a very nice casual wear for girls. It can be wore in parties or while going out with friends. It is best suited with jeans or jeggings. A sling bag and sunglasses will go on with the style.
The price of this beautiful top is around Rs.350.
